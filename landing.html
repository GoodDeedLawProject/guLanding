Nonprofits & Organizers

Elected through peer-review; event organizers are able to quickly organize projects from templates, and set rewards for the work that needs to be done. They can also oversee project fulfillment, approve edits to projects, and reward volunteers. All with one tool.

verified_user

Community Members

The main account. Community members are now able to access available roles in the community, gets points, and redeem them for goods and services in their area. GO Unite is a full ecosystem to help out in the neighborhood, and be rewarded by your neighbors.

fingerprint

Local Businesses

Business owners are able to reward their neighbors and customers for them helping out in the community. They can accepts points, sells goods and services at community functions, and make suggestions for projects that need to be done.
